Location


Location

The 2016 ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing will be held at the Hilton Santa Clara, located in the heart of Silicon Valley.

Getting to the Conference

For those not staying at the Hilton, or local attendees, we encourage taking public transit to attend the conference. In particular, there is a 49'ers game at nearby Levi's Stadium on October 6, which will make parking at the conference very difficult all day. This can also cause road closures (traffic advisory from the City of Santa Clara is available here).

There are VTA Light Rail stops right across the street from the hotel:



Other options, such as taxis, Uber and Lyft, also work well in the Bay Area.

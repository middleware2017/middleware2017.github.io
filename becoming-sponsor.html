Becoming a sponsor

The ACM Symposium on Cloud Computing is the premier research conference on cloud computing. Attendees include top graduate students and premier experts from academia and industry. The 2016 conference will be held in Santa Clara, California in the heart of Silicon Valley, and should attract a variety of attendees from industry and universities. We welcome sponsorship! Sponsoring the conference is an excellent way to attract potential recruits, get visibility for your company and products, and be a part of the cloud computing research community. Please contact Brian Cooper (cooperb at google dot com) to sponsor the conference. Various levels of sponsorship are available.

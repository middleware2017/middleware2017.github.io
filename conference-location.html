ACM/IFIP/USENIX Middleware 2017

Las Vegas, Nevada

Dec 11-15, 2017

Conference Location

The 2017 ACM/IFIP/USENIX Middleware conference will be held at the Student Union of the University of Nevada, Las Vegas (UNLV). All technical sessions, including pre-conference events, will be held on the second floor. Signs will be posted to guide conference attendees. For convenience, the Student Union building also hosts a food court featuring various food vendors, including Starbucks.

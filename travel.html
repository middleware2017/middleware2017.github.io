ACM/IFIP/USENIX Middleware 2017

Las Vegas, Nevada

Dec 11-15, 2017

How to reach Las Vegas

By Plane

The closest airport to the venue is McCarran International Airport (LAS), which is the main airport in the region. There are direct flights from most international cities to LAS. Further, most hotels in the area have airport shuttles connecting LAS to/from the hotels.

By Car

Conference Location

The 2017 ACM/IFIP/USENIX Middleware conference will be held at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas (UNLV). All events will be in the Student Union at UNLV.

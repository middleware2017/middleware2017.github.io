Las Vegas, Nevada

Dec 11-15, 2017

Las Vegas

The conference location is Las Vegas, often simply known as Vegas, and it is a famous and the largest city within the greater Mojave Desert, state of Nevada, USA. The city is an internationally renowned major resort city known primarily for its gambling, entertainment and nightlife. It is one of the top destinations for business conventions and tourism, often called the Entertainment Capital of the World. Weather during December is cool (rarely cold) and cloudy, temperature ranging from 3 to 14℃ (38-57 ℉).

What to visit in Las Vegas


Apart from the major casinos, clubs and resorts along the Las Vegas Strip, the following places may be of interest to the conference attendees:

  1. Marjorie Barrick Museum of Art on UNLV campus
  2. Shark Reef Aquarium at Mandalay Bay about 10 minutes car ride
  3. Adventuredome Theme Park about 12 minutes car ride
  4. Neon Museum about 16 minutes car ride
  5. Red Rock Canyon about 30 minutes car ride
For further information, you can contact Las Vegas Information Center ( 3150 Paradise Rd, Las Vegas, NV 89109, Phone: (877) 847-4858).
